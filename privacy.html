1. Who We Are

Playx ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at playx.io — a free browser gaming platform offering HTML5 games, daily challenges, personality quizzes, and a global leaderboard.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use Playx, how we use it, and what rights you have over your data.

2. What We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you use Playx:

Data TypeWhatWhen
Account InfoName, email, profile pictureWhen you sign in with Google

Usage DataPages visited, time spent, clicksAutomatically (analytics)

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide the service — run games, save your scores, show your rank on the leaderboard
  • Personalization — remember your XP level, daily streak, and preferences
  • Improvement — understand which games and features people use most
  • Communication — send weekly tips if you subscribed (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Security — detect abuse, cheating, or unusual activity
  • Legal compliance — meet our obligations under applicable law

We never use your data for automated decision-making that has legal or significant effects on you.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Playx uses the following technologies to store data:

  • localStorage — stores your XP, streak, best scores, and game preferences locally on your device. This never leaves your browser unless you're signed in.
  • Session cookies — keep you logged in during your visit (cleared when you close the browser).
  • Analytics cookies — anonymous usage statistics to improve the site (via Google Analytics).
  • Advertising cookies — placed by Google AdSense to show relevant ads (see Section 5).

You can clear all local data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage and cookies. This will reset your offline progress.

5. Advertising (Google AdSense)

Playx displays advertisements served by Google AdSense to keep the platform free for everyone. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you ads based on your interests.

Google's advertising practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com.

Note: We display ads to fund free access to all games and features. Playx Pro subscribers see no ads.

We comply with the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework and applicable data protection laws including GDPR and CCPA.

6. Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive your:

  • Name and email address
  • Profile picture (public Google account photo)
  • A unique Google user ID (to identify your account)

We do not receive your Google password, contacts, calendar, or any other Google data. You can revoke Playx's access at any time via Google Account Permissions.

Guest players (not signed in) have no account data stored on our servers — only in their browser's localStorage.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database provider, for storing your account, scores, and game history. Data is stored on EU servers. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google — for authentication (Google Sign-In) and advertising (AdSense). Google Privacy Policy
  • Law enforcement — only if required by a valid legal request.

Public leaderboard data (username and score) is visible to all Playx users by design.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account data — kept as long as your account is active
  • Game scores and XP — kept indefinitely (they're your achievements)
  • Usage analytics — anonymized after 14 months
  • Deleted accounts — all personal data removed within 30 days of deletion request
9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Withdrawal — withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@playx.io. We respond within 30 days.

CCPA (California): California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. We do not sell personal information. Contact us to submit a CCPA request.

GDPR (EU/UK): EU and UK residents may lodge a complaint with their local data protection authority if unsatisfied with our response.

10. Children's Privacy

Playx is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@playx.io and we will delete it promptly.

Users between 13–16 in the EU require parental consent for data processing under GDPR.

11. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify signed-in users via email for significant changes
  • Show a notice on the Playx homepage for 7 days after major updates

Continued use of Playx after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
